Privacy Policy

SipBloom — Water Reminder by Aditanix Studios

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Summary: SipBloom stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The only third-party service used is Google AdMob for displaying advertisements.

1. Information We Collect

SipBloom is designed with privacy in mind. The app collects the following information, which is stored only on your device:

  • Profile Information: Weight, age, gender, activity level, and climate preference — used solely to calculate your personalized daily hydration goal.
  • Water Intake Data: The amount and type of beverages you log, along with timestamps — used to track your hydration progress.
  • App Preferences: Reminder settings, daily goal, notification preferences, and gamification progress (plant level, streaks).

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Photos, files, or media
  • Any data from other apps on your device

2. How We Use Your Information

All data is used exclusively within the app to:

  • Calculate your personalized daily water intake goal
  • Display your hydration progress and history
  • Send you hydration reminders at your preferred times
  • Track streaks, grow your virtual plant, and provide motivational insights

3. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using Android's Room database and DataStore preferences. No data is transmitted to any external server. If you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted.

4. Third-Party Services

SipBloom uses the following third-party service:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the free version of the app. AdMob may collect certain information to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data for ad performance

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your device's Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Children's Privacy

SipBloom is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is rated for general audiences and does not contain any age-restricted content.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security features (screen lock, encryption). We do not have access to your data at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete daily data: Use the "Reset today's data" option in Settings.
  • Delete all data: Uninstall the app to remove all stored data permanently.
  • Control ads: Opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
